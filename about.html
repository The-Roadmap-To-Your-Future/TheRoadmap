About The Roadmap

The Roadmap is a student-run publication based at the Lawrenceville School dedicated to highlighting the diverse academic, extracurricular, and personal journeys of students across disciplines. Through interviews, features, and reflections, we aim to illuminate the paths that lead from curiosity to purpose.

Our Mission

We believe that learning from the experiences of others can help students navigate their own futures with confidence and clarity. By sharing honest stories, practical advice, and thoughtful perspectives, The Roadmap serves as a guide for students exploring who they are and who they want to become.
